About Subhash Chandra Bose

Subhas Chandra Bose was an Indian nationalist leader who played a key role in India's struggle for independence against British rule. He was born on January 23, 1897, in Cuttack, Odisha. Bose is known for his famous slogan, "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!"
